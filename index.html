What is Philippine Smart Bus?

The Philippines' first autonomous bus service, launched in New Clark City, Clark Freeport Zone, and Clark International Airport.

Key features include:

  • Autonomous Operation: Self-driving technology powered by Japanese systems
  • Free Rides: Currently offering complimentary service until fare regulations are established
  • Smart Technology: Utilizes Smart Mobility Operation Cloud (SMOC) for efficient operations
  • Sustainable: Part of a low-carbon transportation ecosystem

The Philippine Smart Bus in operation at New Clark City

How Does It Work?

Smart Bus navigating through Clark Freeport Zone

The Philippine Smart Bus operates through advanced autonomous technology:

  1. Last-Mile Service: Connects key areas within New Clark City and surrounding zones
  2. Demand-Based Dispatch: Uses SMOC to optimize vehicle deployment based on real-time demand
  3. Integrated Ecosystem: Works with electric vehicles, e-bikes, and smart infrastructure

The system minimizes the number of vehicles needed while maximizing coverage through intelligent routing and scheduling algorithms.

When Was It Introduced?

The Philippine Smart Bus was officially launched on September 10, 2024, with a ribbon-cutting ceremony in New Clark City.

Development timeline:

  • January 2023: Memorandum of Understanding with NEDO
  • October 2023: Agreement signed with BCDA, Zenmov, and MC Metro
  • September 2024: Official launch and public demonstration

The demonstration program is scheduled to run until June 2025, funded by NEDO and Zenmov.

Launch ceremony of the Philippine Smart Bus

Where Is It Already Implemented?

Smart Bus route map in Clark area

Current implementation areas:

  • New Clark City: Primary deployment area as part of smart city initiative
  • Clark Freeport Zone: Connecting business and residential areas
  • Clark International Airport: Providing transportation links

The service aims to reduce traffic congestion, improve accessibility, and support the development of New Clark City as the Philippines' first sustainable smart city.
